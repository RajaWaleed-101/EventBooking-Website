18th Oct 2025

London Tech Hub

Shaping the future of healthcare at the Global Health Summit

Global Health Summit

1st Nov 2025

Geneva Convention Center

Discussions on healthcare, AI in medicine, and global health challenges. Join leading experts in addressing the most pressing health issues of our time.

The Global Health Summit brings together healthcare professionals, researchers, policymakers, and technology innovators to discuss the future of healthcare and explore solutions to global health challenges. Celebrating cinematic excellence at the Film Gala Awards

Film Gala Awards

12th Nov 2025

Dolby Theatre, Los Angeles

A night of glamour celebrating the best in international cinema. Join filmmakers, actors, and industry professionals for a prestigious award ceremony honoring excellence in filmmaking.

The Film Gala Awards recognizes outstanding achievements in various categories, including directing, acting, screenwriting, cinematography, and more. The ceremony will feature red carpet arrivals, live performances, memorable acceptance speeches, and exclusive after-parties.
